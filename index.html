FIZZBUZZ!

The FizzBuzz game! A great way to practice multiplication, or a awesome party game.

How to play? its really easy. if you know how to multiply by 3 and 5, and if you dont, this game will help you learn!

  1. how many players? As many players as you want! Sit in a circle.
  2. turns are clockwised.
  3. the youngest player goes first by saying "1" each player continues counting one number a turn, However, any number divisible by three is replaced by the word fizz and any divisible by five by the word buzz.
  4. Numbers divisible by both become fizz buzz.
  5. A player who hesitates or makes a mistake is eliminated from the game.
